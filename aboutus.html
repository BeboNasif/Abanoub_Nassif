About Us



Since our inception, at Amesh.USA we have always felt committed to protection, seeking the best solutions for the most adverse fire conditions.


This philosophy of overcoming increasingly greater challenges has helped us grow and develop, always striving to provide the highest level of quality in each of our projects.


Thanks to the tireless work of continuous improvement, we have become one of the fastest growing companies in the industry in recent years. We have made significant strides from being a national benchmark (USA) to building a significant international presence.


Advancing from being a national benchmark to building a significant international presence.


We can offer the best solution by tailoring our specially developed solutions for each hazard. Our technical team always advises you on all the options appropriate to each protection, studying the solution that best suits the unique characteristics of each hazard.


It should be noted that products included in Amesh.USA offers are selected based exclusively on technical and quality criteria. Since Amesh.USA is not tied to any particular methodology of fire protection or brand, we can offer our customers the most effective product, without leaning towards a specific solution. We manufacture and distribute systems that protect via water, water mist, foam, dry chemical powders, smoke & fire control, electronic detection, clean agents, and other gases.


Confidence in our firefighting equipment is due to our philosophy of continuous improvement. Our large investment in R&D and Innovation has resulted in the development of the most advanced technology. This has led us to obtain the largest number of certificates and approvals from international organizations, backing our commitment to quality.



Our values



Environmentally friendly.



Today, humanity is aware of how fragile the planet is. It is crucial to take all necessary measures to preserve our atmosphere. Government agencies, including Amesh.USA, have decided to limit substances that affect the environment. However, one of the most serious problems stems from uncontrolled fires over large areas that often contain materials that give off toxic fumes.



These hazards need to be protected with quick and effective means that minimize damage. Amesh.USA is well aware of these problems. Therefore, we work with fast-acting and environmentally friendly products, ensuring that the extinguishing effects will always be beneficial for the planet.



Providing the safety required in this regard is one of our main objectives. This is why we go a step further, offering the widest range of inert gas systems in the market. These gases come from the atmosphere and thus have no adverse effect on it. Full-scale tests can be performed without any damage to the atmosphere.
